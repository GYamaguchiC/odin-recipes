Strogonoff

Decription

Strogonoff is a creamy and savory dish made with tender pieces of meat, typically chicken or beef, cooked in a rich sauce of cream, tomato, and seasonings. Popular in Brazil and inspired by Russian cuisine, it's usually served with white rice and crispy potato sticks, making it a comforting and flavorful meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken brest, cut into small cubes
  • 1 small onion, finelly chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 table spoon vegetable oil or butter
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 200ml heavy cream or sour cream
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken by seasoning it with salt and pepper
  2. Cook the aromatics
  3. Cook the chicken
  4. Add flavoring such as mustard
  5. Lower the heat and add the heavy cream
  6. Serve wth white rice and potatos sticks for traditional Brazilian style
